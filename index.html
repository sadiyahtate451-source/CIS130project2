About Ari Patrinos

Name: Ari Patrinos

Office Seeking: City Controller of Philadelphia

Political Party: Republican

Biography: A Northwest Philadelphia native, Ari Patrinos brings a wealth of experience to the table. He is a proud graduate of Harvard University and holds a Master's in Political Science from the University of Chicago. His professional career includes years as a successful Wall Street stockbroker, where he honed his skills in finance and fiscal management. Ari is committed to bringing accountability and a fresh, results-driven approach to the City Controller's office.
