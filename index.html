Justin Pearson

Office: U.S. House of Representatives

Party: Democratic

Justin Pearson is an American politician and activist known for his public service, particularly in Tennessee, and his advocacy for education, economic equality, and social justice. He gained national attention after being expelled from the Tennessee House of Representatives for protesting gun violence and has focused on environmental justice and empowering youth voices. Pearson's work includes co-founding the environmental organization Memphis Community Against Pollution (MCAP) and his commitment to education policy reform, as demonstrated by his time on the Youth Advisory Board.
